About EthioScholar AI

EthioScholar AI is an open educational utility constructed to deliver high-quality academic support to Ethiopian high school and university students.

Engineered using pure native web infrastructure for instantaneous load times even over slower mobile data layers across Ethiopian networks.

Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters. EthioScholar AI handles your questions with absolute discretion:

  • Conversations are preserved entirely inside your browser's local sandbox environment via Local Storage.
  • No telemetry, profiling, tracking pixels, or cookie structures are embedded.
  • API interactions pass directly to the language model processor without long-term server retention pipelines.
